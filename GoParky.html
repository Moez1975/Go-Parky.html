Go Parky

Parky collects comprehensive real-time data on the parking lot so that administrators can have an insight into real-time. It is a responsive solution, so parking owners, operators and administrators can make changes instantly with a few clicks on their computer, tablet, or mobile device from anywhere.

The ‘Smart Parking’ app is available to download for both Android and iOS, and contains a raft of features designed to help direct vistors to available parking spaces, advise of car park conditions and tariffs and allow to utilise contactless payment
